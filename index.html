Brief Bio

I am a Principal Researcher at Samsung Advanced Institute of Technology(SAIT), Suwon, South Korea and I am a Technical Lead of Autonomous Driving Team at SAIT. Prior working at SAIT, I was a Principle Engineer at Automotive SOC Team, System LSI Division, Samsung Electronics in Hwaseong, South Korea, and before that I was a Visiting Researcher at Montreal Institute for Learning Algorithms(MILA), working under the supervision of Professor Yoshua Bengio, Professor Simon Lacoste-Julien. I completed my PhD at POSTECH(Pohang University of Science and Technology) under the supervision of Professor Sangchul Won. During my Ph.D. I also closely collaborated with Professor Juhyun Park(Yeungnam University) and Sangmoon Lee(Kyungbook National University). My current and past research work broadly involve machine learning, computer vision, robotics and control. More recently, I am primarily interested in connected, autonomous driving, mobility, electric in the field of automotive technology.
